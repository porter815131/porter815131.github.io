Preparation of the Phone Interview

1. (Introduce)Tell me a little bit about yourself

  • I graduated from (University) with a (bachelor degree) degree in (major) in (year)
  • I was in charge of (n, n and n)
  • I assisted with…
  • I was responsible for…
  • My duties include…
  • I was in that job/position for (number) years. Then I had the opportunity to be the (supervisor) for (XXX) company.
  • Then I went on to be (position) for (XXX) company.

My name is Jerry. I have over 8 years of experience in the warehouse & logistic industry in Taiwan. My first career that is related to warehousing is as a warehouse assistant with lativ company. I was in charge of processing daily orders and unpacking shipping containers. I worked with them for over 3 years. After that, I went to Australia on a working holiday for a year. (All of my work on working holiday was related to agriculture, like harvesting fruits or packing vegetables) When I came back to Taiwan, I went on to be Warehouse Storeperson for HCT company. I was responsible for inventory management and Carrying out tasks with high-reach forklift. I was in that job for 4 years. Then I worked for the LF company for about 1.5 years. My duties in that company include delivery arrangement, operating high-reach forklift ,and operating RF scanner to pick orders. Besides, I’ve obtained the Australian High Risk Work Licence. I have the skills and qualities to match the job desrciption. Outside of work I keep myself fit and active. I will go to the gym or hiking in my free time.

2. (Accomplishment)What is the biggest accomplishment from these jobs?

  • After the (something you did or plan), we saw a (how many percetage) increase in (which things gets benefits)
  • Though the effort of our team, we successfully (did something)

When I was still working at HCT company, our team simplified the workflow for picking and packing orders. We optimized every step of the work. Then we saw a 12% increase in Order fulfillment rate.

3-1. (Weakness)What is your biggest weakness?

I think I’m good at planning. My motto is “working smarter rather than harder”. When we were going to process the daily orders, the supervisor would discuss the process with me. Then we tried and got very good results, we saved a lot of effort and manpower.

3-2. (Weakness)What is your biggest weakness?

  • I’m too much of a perfertionist.
  • I have a hard time balancing work and life. I think I work too hard sometime.

I’m too much of a perfertionist. I have a hard time balancing work and life. I think I work too hard sometime.

4. (Why this company)What do you like about company?

  • When I was browsing your website, I really liked your career training program for your employees. I really like that you are care about your employee’s growth. That is really important to me.

When I was browsing your website, I really liked your company’s friendliness and tolerance for cultural diversity. I really like that you are care about your employee’s growth. That is really important to me.

5. (Why hire you)Why do you think you’ll be a good candidate for this position?

(Discuss the job duties(1~3) they posted)

  • Like I said, I was in charge of (forklift) for (8) years. I can hit the ground running. I know a great deal about (warehousing). So based on all that, I think this position is right up my alley and I have a lot to offer to your company.

As I metioned before, I have experience of operating forklift for years. I can hit the ground running. I know a great deal about warehousing, like picking and packing orders, operating RF scanner. So based on all that, I think this position is right up my alley and I have a lot to offer to your company.

6. (Leaving previous JOB)Why are you leaving your current job/position?

  • I’ve had an amazing experience in this job. I’ve learned a lot. But I think I’m ready for the next step.I’m ready for a bigger challenge.

I’ve had an amazing experience in this job. I’ve learned a lot. But I would go to Australia to study, so I have to leave.

7. (Thanks)In the end of phone interview

  • Thank you so much for your call. I really apperciate you taking the time to interview me. Based on what I’ve heard, I’m really interested in this position and I think I have a lot to offer. I would be pleased to meet you in person to talk more. Have a great day!
