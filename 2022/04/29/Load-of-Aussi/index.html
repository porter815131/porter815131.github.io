Aussi Expressions

  1. Are you kidding?
  2. As if!
  3. That’s epic!
  4. No worries!
  5. Tough luck
  6. Catch ya(later)
  7. Sorry
  8. What’s going on?
  9. Chugging along
  10. I’m wrecked
  11. I’m going to bail

1. Are you kidding?

Are you joking?
Are you serious?
For real?
Fair dinkum? (Slang)

2. As if!

To show disbelief, to show that you don’t belief something is true, that you doubt something.

  • As if that would be the case
  • As if that is true

EX: As if I don’t like chocolate. (好像我不喜歡巧克力一樣)

3. That’s epic!

To show shock and superise that something is awesome, something is incredible.

  • That’s awesome
  • That rocks
  • That’s great
  • That’s phenomenal
  • That’s incredible

4. No worries!

  • Not to worry
  • Not a problem
  • All good
  • There’s no issued
  • No wukkas (No fucking worries)(Slang)

5. Tough luck

  • Too bad
  • Deal with it
  • I don’t care
  • What a shame

6. Catch ya(later)

  • Goodbye
  • See you later
  • See ya

7. Sorry

  • Pardon?
  • What did you say?
  • Could you please repeat that?

8. What’s going on?

What’s happening? What’s your news? [^tip]

[^tip]: When we replay to greeting that start with “what”, the response is generally a negated response. ex: not much, not a lot, nothing.

  • What’s going on with you?
  • What’s going on in your life?
  • What’s happening?
  • How have you been up to?
  • S’going on? (Slang)

9. Chugging along

Uesd to replay to the greeting, like “How are you?”, “How are you going?”, “How are you doing?”

It kind of like I’m continuing to do something in the same way as before.

10. I’m wrecked (Slang)

  • I’m stuffed
  • I’m cactus
  • I’m buggered
  • I’m fatigued
  • I’m tired

11. I’m going to bail

To bail means “to leave”

  • I’m going to leave
  • I’mena bail (Slang)

12. Chuck a U-ey

Make a U-turn

  • Can I chuck a uey here?

13. Not here to Fuck Spiders

Stop wasting time or “not here to fuck around. I am here to get the job done”

  • “Mate look at this” " Hey, we are not here to Fuck Spiders, get back to it."
